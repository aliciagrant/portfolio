about me

I am a Computer Science student at the University of Florida with a minor in Psychology, aspiring to grow in user experience design and product management. My goal is to enhance my UI/UX, technical, and product strategy skill sets through meaningful opportunities.

The "skip intro" button, after hours of binge-watching TV, sparked more than just a moment of appreciation—it was a realization. This small feature inspired me to pursue UX design, showing me how impactful and thoughtful design can improve everyday experiences.
